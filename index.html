What are Beecons?

Beecons are small wireless sensors that you can use in any location or object. They broadcast small radio signals which your smartphone can receive and interpret, unlocking micro-location and awareness.

With BEEJUY apps downloaded, your smartphone is able to understand their proximity to nearby locations and objects, recognizing their type by a singular ID, approximate location, and its motion. This allow us to build a new generation of mobile apps that connects the real world with your smartphone or tablet, giving you endless opportunities as a shopper or brand.
